THE INTERNET

The Internet is one of the most valuble contributions that the study of Computer Science has brought to society. There are many invaluble apllications of the internet which improve the lives of billions around the world every single day.

  • Education
  • Communication
  • Community
  • Awareness

Education

A good freely available education is taken for granted in many countries. There are countries around the world such as Niger where only 17% of girls aged 15-24 are literate or Liberia where 2/3 of primary school children drop of out school. The Internet allows anyone to get access to any information they wish to find. There are websites such as Wikipedia which is a very reliable free source of information which allow anyone to learn easily.

The Internet also creates opportunites to gain a full education without having to physically go to school for example the Open University. There are thousands of paid courses which allow anyone at any age to get qualifications.

Communication

Origianlly the Internet was only used by Universities to share data and findings. Now anyone can use the Internet to communicate with people from any country and timezone around the world. This is still very useful for sharing information but also useful for families and friends to stay connected when they do not live close to eachother.

Websistes such as Facebook and Twitter allow people to message eachother for free and share other content such as images, videos and GIFs. E-mail is proably the msot common way people communicate over the internet. 306 billion E-mails are sent around the world daily. The internet allows businessess to negotiate without ever having to meet in person wich saves a lot of time.

Community

The internet enables communities of like-minded people from around the world to connect and shared their interests. This is particularly useful for people who have niche interets that cannot usually share their passion with others. The Internet has so many users that there is bound to be another person that shares your passion.

Awareness

The Internet allows anyone to share information on social media sites such as Facebook and Instagram. This allows people to spread awraeness of issues not only locally but in the wider community and even about foreign countries. Donation pages and petition sites can also be set up in order for anyone to have a say/impact on issues around the globe. Sites like this include Change.org and Just Giving
